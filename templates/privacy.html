Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when using this Medical Chatbot. This may include questions you ask, chat history, and any other information you choose to share.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services. Your chat history is stored temporarily in memory to enable conversational context during your session.

3. Data Storage and Security

Chat session data is stored in memory and is not persisted to disk or shared with third parties. We recommend not sharing sensitive personal health information through this chatbot.

4. Third-Party Services

This service uses Pinecone for vector storage and HuggingFace for language model inference. Your questions may be processed through these services to generate responses.

5. Cookies and Analytics

This website does not use cookies or tracking analytics. Each session is identified by a session ID stored in browser memory.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. Since session data is stored only in memory, refreshing your browser will clear your chat history.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
